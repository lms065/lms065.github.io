Who are the officers for LaSCO?

LaSCO is a "club", but there are no officers. Our sponsor for the club is Ms. Linda Gleason, who is a math teacher at LSMSA. For any information you can either use the email link above or go to her office for more information, or just find someone who is currently in LaSCO, as we should be able to answer your basic questions about the club and help you find your way to the meetings
